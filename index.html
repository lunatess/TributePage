Planking

The 2011 trend that shouldn't have died (RIP)

About

Planking is a photo trend which involves lying face down with arms to the sides in public spaces, often in weird locations (e.g., on top of a lampost) and then photographing the scene and sharing the image online. Planking quickly became popular across the world in 2011 being shared to various sites including facebook. Various celebrities, such as Katy Perry, Justin Bieber, Demi Moore, Chris Brown and Rosario Dawson also joined in on the trend

Planking timeline:

  • On March 27th, 2011, rugby player David "Wolfman" Williams planked after a try during the Manly-Warringah Sea Eagles vs Newcastle Knights game
  • On May 13th, 2011, American journalist Michelle McMurray wrote an article titled "First Annual Global Planking Day: May 25, 2011" calling on readers to join the inaugural holiday of planking
  • On May 15th, 2011, the planking trend became a controversial topic in the news media when Acton Beale, a 20 year old man, fell to his death from an attempt at planking on a seventh floor balcony is Brisbane, Australia
  • On July 6th, 2011, Rapper Xzibit expressed his hate for planking on Twitter calling it "the dumbest shit ever" and claiming that the trend was related to slavery
  • On July 12th, 2011, former MTV host and comedian Tom Green uploaded a YouTube video titled "Tom Green Created Planking – 1994" depicting one of his early pranks from his Canadian local-access TV show that resembled planking
  • On July 28th, fans at the Grauman's Chinese Theater in Los Angeles for the premiere of the documentary "Electronic Daisy Carnival Experience" planked in protest in front of the police
  • On August 16th, 2011, American R&B singer Chris Brown referenced planking in a tweet in which he said: "No more planking for me unless it’s on a sexy lady! Lol."
